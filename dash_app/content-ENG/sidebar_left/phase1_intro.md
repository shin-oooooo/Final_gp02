This phase defines **model-failure boundaries** with hard statistical thresholds **before** running predictive models.

Covariance **structural-entropy** monitoring quantifies early signs of **collective impairment** across asset buckets
when cohorts behave alike and classical hedges fail.

The **ADF** test detects **unit roots**—a prerequisite for treating a series as stationary.

The **Ljung–Box** test is the standard check that data behave as **white noise** (no serial correlation).
